# Anti-AI Prose Checklist

Use this checklist after the substantive argument is already correct.

## Check for Mechanical Patterns

- several consecutive sentences with nearly identical length
- repeated sentence openings
- too many abstract verbs such as "influence", "impact", or "play a role"
- paragraph flow that sounds like headings turned into prose
- frequent reliance on quotation marks, colons, and dashes for emphasis

## Revise Toward

- mixed sentence lengths
- specific verbs that match the evidence
- transitions that emerge from logic rather than stock phrases
- concrete statements of what the literature finds
- prose that sounds like explanation, not assembly

## Fast Test

Read the paragraph aloud.

If it sounds as if every sentence was produced from the same template, rewrite the openings and sentence rhythm before returning it.
